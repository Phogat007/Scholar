Scholar offers a comprehensive collection of previous year question papers across various courses and disciplines. We also keep you updated with the latest news and notifications from the college.

As a fellow MCA student from Subodh PG College, I understand how challenging it can be to manage college demands. That's why I created this platform to make things easier for you. Here, you can access previous year question papers, important college updates, and other valuable resources. I hope these materials help you with your exams and give you a clearer idea of what to expect.

We have an extensive library of question papers from diverse courses and disciplines, serving as essential study aids. We continually expand our collection to ensure you have access to papers from various years and semesters. Stay informed with the latest college notifications, including exam schedules, timetables, and significant announcements, through our centralized hub.

Recognizing the overwhelming nature of student life, we also offer additional resources such as subject-specific notes, study guides, and practical tips to enhance your academic experience. Rely on our support to excel in your college endeavors with confidence!
