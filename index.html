Discover

Scholar offers a comprehensive collection of previous year question papers across various courses and disciplines. We keep you informed with the latest updates and notifications from the college.

As a team of dedicated MCA Studentsof Graphic Era University, we recognize the challenges and demands of college life. Having been juniors ourselves, we understand the importance of having essential resources for academic achievement. Our aim is to provide a one-stop platform for all your academic needs. Access previous year question papers, crucial college notifications, and a wealth of other resources. These materials will aid in your exam preparation and offer valuable insights into exam patterns.

We have an extensive library of question papers from diverse courses and disciplines, serving as indispensable study aids. We continuously expand our repository to ensure you have access to papers from various years and semesters. Stay informed with the latest college notifications, including exam schedules, timetables, and significant announcements through our centralized hub.

Understanding the overwhelming nature of student life, we also offer additional resources such as subject-specific notes, study guides, and practical tips to enhance your academic experience. Rely on our support and excel in your college endeavors with confidence!
